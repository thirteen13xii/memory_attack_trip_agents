# China Cultural & Culinary Journey: A 7-Day Luxury Itinerary

**Prepared for:** Valued Client  
**Travel Dates:** 7-Day Itinerary  
**Consultant:** Your Personal Travel Advisor  
**Theme:** Imperial History, Culinary Excellence & Natural Wonders

Welcome! This meticulously crafted journey takes you through the heart of China, blending ancient history with modern luxury, world-class cuisine, and breathtaking landscapes. You'll experience the grandeur of Beijing, the archaeological wonders of Xi'an, the serene beauty of Guilin, and the vibrant pulse of Shanghai. Every detail has been considered to ensure a seamless, enriching, and unforgettable adventure.

---

## **Day 1: Beijing – Arrival & Imperial Ambiance**

*   **Afternoon (2:00 PM): Arrival & Settling In**
    *   **Activity:** Check into the **Aman at Summer Palace**. This isn't just a hotel; it's an experience. Nestled beside the UNESCO World Heritage site, your traditional courtyard accommodation offers a serene escape. Take a moment to unwind in the peaceful gardens, feeling the imperial history that permeates the air.
    *   **Pro Tip:** If you have energy, the Aman can arrange a private, after-hours tour of the Summer Palace grounds for a truly magical and crowd-free experience.

*   **Evening (7:00 PM): A Peking Duck Masterclass**
    *   **Dining:** Dinner at **Da Dong Roast Duck**. Renowned for its innovation, Da Dong serves what many consider the pinnacle of Peking duck—incredibly crispy skin with minimal fat. The theatrical carving at your table is part of the show.

*   **Night (9:00 PM): The Art of Opera**
    *   **Activity:** Attend the **Beijing Opera at Liyuan Theatre**. With its vibrant costumes, intricate makeup, and stylized movements, this performance is a captivating introduction to traditional Chinese performing arts. The theatre's intimate setting makes it perfect for visitors.

---

## **Day 2: Beijing – Monuments of Epochs**

*   **Morning (10:00 AM): Walk Upon History**
    *   **Activity:** Visit the **Great Wall**. I recommend a section like Mutianyu for its stunning views and restored, accessible walkways. Standing on this ancient marvel, you'll grasp the sheer scale of China's historical ambition. Allow time for the cable car ride and a peaceful walk along the ramparts.
    *   **Advisor's Note:** Wear comfortable, sturdy shoes. The steps are uneven and can be steep.

*   **Afternoon (3:00 PM): The Heart of the Empire**
    *   **Activity:** Explore the **Forbidden City**. Wander through the series of majestic gates and vast courtyards, imagining the life of emperors and their courts within these vermilion walls. The scale is immense, so focus on the central axis and key halls like the Hall of Supreme Harmony.
    *   **Lunch Suggestion:** Consider a quick, local lunch near the Forbidden City. Your driver can recommend a spot for simple noodles or dumplings.

*   **Evening:** *At your leisure.* Perhaps enjoy a cocktail at the Aman's bar or explore the nearby Hutongs (ancient alleyways) by rickshaw for a glimpse of old Beijing.

---

## **Day 3: To Xi'an – Ancient Capitals & Dumplings**

*   **Morning (10:00 AM): Journey West**
    *   **Travel:** Domestic flight from **Beijing to Xi'an**. You'll be met at Xi'an airport and transferred to your hotel.

*   **Afternoon (1:00 PM): Check into History**
    *   **Accommodation:** Check into the **Sofitel Legend People's Grand Hotel Xian**. This hotel is a landmark itself, with a grand, historic atmosphere that perfectly complements your stay in this ancient capital.

*   **Evening (7:00 PM): A Dumpling Banquet**
    *   **Dining:** Dinner at **Defachang Dumpling Restaurant**. This is a feast for the eyes and palate. You'll sample an incredible variety of dumplings (jiaozi), each with different fillings, shapes, and flavors—a true Xi'an specialty.

*   **Night (9:00 PM): Tang Dynasty Spectacle**
    *   **Activity:** Watch the **Tang Dynasty Music and Dance Show**. This colorful and elegant performance, with musicians and dancers in period costume, brings to life the glorious art of one of China's most prosperous dynasties.

---

## **Day 4: Xi'an – Warriors & Walls**

*   **Morning (9:00 AM): The Silent Army**
    *   **Activity:** Visit the **Terracotta Army**. The sheer scale of this archaeological discovery is humbling. Take your time in Pit 1, the largest, to appreciate the rows of unique, life-sized warriors. A knowledgeable guide is highly recommended here to unlock the stories behind the figures.
    *   **Advisor's Note:** The site is about an hour from the city. We'll arrange a private car and guide for maximum comfort and insight.

*   **Afternoon (2:00 PM): Ancient Cityscape**
    *   **Activity:** Explore **Xi'an's Ancient City Wall and Bell Tower**. You can rent a bicycle and ride along the top of the remarkably well-preserved Ming Dynasty wall for fantastic city views. The Bell Tower marks the city's geographical and historical center.

*   **Evening:** *At your leisure.* Explore the vibrant Muslim Quarter near the Drum Tower for street food and market atmosphere.

---

## **Day 5: To Guilin – Karst Peaks & River Lights**

*   **Morning (10:00 AM): Southward Bound**
    *   **Travel:** Domestic flight from **Xi'an to Guilin**.

*   **Afternoon (1:00 PM): A Lakeside Retreat**
    *   **Accommodation:** Check into the **Shangri-La Hotel, Guilin**. Your room will offer postcard-perfect views of the iconic limestone karst peaks. Take a deep breath—the pace here is serene.

*   **Evening (7:00 PM): Flavors of the Li River**
    *   **Dining:** Dinner at **Jiu Ma Hua Fish Restaurant**. Savor the freshness of local Li River fish, prepared with Guilin's signature flavors. It's the perfect culinary prelude to tomorrow's cruise.

*   **Night (9:00 PM): A Natural Theatre**
    *   **Activity:** Watch the **Impression Sanjie Liu Light Show**. Directed by Zhang Yimou, this outdoor spectacle uses the Li River itself as a stage and the karst mountains as a natural backdrop. It's an awe-inspiring fusion of light, song, and landscape.

---

## **Day 6: River Serenity & Shanghai Glamour**

*   **Morning (9:00 AM): The Paintings Come to Life**
    *   **Activity:** Embark on a **Li River cruise**. This is the essence of Guilin. As you drift down the river, you'll pass through a dreamscape of verdant peaks, water buffalo, and bamboo groves—scenes that have inspired Chinese poets and painters for centuries.
    *   **Advisor's Note:** The cruise typically lasts 4-5 hours and includes lunch on board. Pack light, but don't forget your camera!

*   **Afternoon (3:00 PM): To the Futuristic Metropolis**
    *   **Travel:** Domestic flight from **Guilin to Shanghai**.

*   **Evening (6:00 PM): Arrival on The Bund**
    *   **Accommodation:** Check into **The Peninsula Shanghai**. Located right on the historic Bund, your Art Deco-inspired room offers breathtaking views of the Pudong skyline across the Huangpu River.

*   **Night (8:00 PM): Elegant Shanghai Supper**
    *   **Dining:** Dinner at **Fu 1088**. Housed in a restored colonial villa, this restaurant offers an intimate and sophisticated setting for classic Shanghainese dishes like braised pork and drunken chicken. It's a culinary step back into the glamour of old Shanghai.

---

## **Day 7: Shanghai – The Bund & Silver Screen**

*   **Morning (10:00 AM): Where East Meets West**
    *   **Activity:** Stroll along **The Bund**. This iconic waterfront promenade showcases Shanghai's architectural history, with grand European-style buildings on one side and the futuristic towers of Pudong on the other. It's the perfect place for a morning walk and photos.

*   **Afternoon (2:00 PM): A Taste of Cinema**
    *   **Activity:** Attend the **Shanghai International Film Festival**. Immerse yourself in the glamour and creativity of Asia's premier film event. You might catch a screening, a panel discussion, or simply soak in the festival atmosphere around the city.
    *   **Advisor's Note:** Specific event details and tickets will be secured for you based on the final festival schedule.

*   **Evening (7:00 PM): Farewell**
    *   **Travel:** Private transfer to Shanghai Pudong International Airport (PVG) for your **departure flight**, concluding your remarkable journey through China.
